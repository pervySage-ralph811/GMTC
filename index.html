Gingoog Manpower Training Center
Baybay, Lunao, Gingoog City

INTRODUCTION

Gingoog Manpower training Center (GMTC) located at Purok #3, Baybay Lunao, Gingoog City is a technical vocational training center owned and operated by the City government of Gingoog, Misamis Oriental. It's creation was under Sangguniang Panglunsod Resolution No. 2008-196, Series of 2008.

The GMTC's existence was brought about by the increasing demand for globally competitive skilled workers. The GMTC has been authorized and certified by TESDA as a credible implenenting institution for severaltraining programs and gradutes. Its credibility were backed-up by TESDA Registered Programs Single and Bundled Qualifications in Construction sector.

GMTC has been continuously and consistently providing useful Technical Education and Skills Development, Livelihood, Scholarship and Community-Based Training Programs to all interested locals. This initiative is in line with one of the Gingoog City's Strategic Directions "GOLD" under the D Delivery og Health, Education and Social Services.

VISSION

To become the center of quality Technical-Vocational training for middle-level workforce in the city and neighboring municipalities by providing an avenue for progress in laying down the foundations of a cognizable start.

MISSION

GINGOOG MANPOWER TRAINING CENTER committed to provide quality TECH-VOC training programs through competency- based & dual training system at the same time develop proper work values and attitude for the development of world class workers and assist employment opportunities to help alleviate the living condition of its constituents.

OFFICEMANDATE

Promote Technical Education and Skills Development programs and maximizes utilization of Technical Capabilities of the middle-level manpower workforce through provision of : Skills Trainings, Technical Vocational Scholarship Programs, Competency Assessment and Certification and Career Guidance.

OFFICE GOALS

1. Highly employable graduates in both local and foreign labor markets.

2. Improved productivity of workers that can contribute significantly to the growth of the economy.

3. Effective management of Training Center Operations.

4. Efficient implementation of Training Center Programs.
